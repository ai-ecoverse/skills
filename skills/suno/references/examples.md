## Example 1: Operatic Rockabilly with Satirical Lyrics

**Title**: Crazy Little Problematic Tom

**Style**: `Operatic rockabilly, fast-paced, upbeat, male vocals, hymnic chorus, energetic`

```
[Intro]
Oh yeah, oh yeah!
Here's a story 'bout a man
With a smile that lights the screen
But behind those perfect teeth
There's something quite obscene!

[Verse 1]
He jumps from planes and scales the walls
Does his own stunts, never falls
Box office gold, that charming grin
But wait until you see what lies within!

[Chorus]
He's a crazy little problematic man called Tom
(Oh Tom, oh Tom!)
Running fast but can't outrun where he's from
(Where's he from? Where's he from?)
Got a couch he likes to jump upon
He's a crazy little problematic man called Tom!

[Verse 2]
Action hero, leading man
But follows quite a different plan
When the cameras stop their roll
He's got a very different goal!

[Bridge]
Short in stature, tall in fame
Playing everybody's game
Science fiction's not just on the screen
If you know what I mean!
(Oh yeah!)

[Chorus]
He's a crazy little problematic man called Tom
(Oh Tom, oh Tom!)
Running fast but can't outrun where he's from
(Where's he from? Where's he from?)
Got a couch he likes to jump upon
He's a crazy little problematic man called Tom!

[Outro]
Rock and roll with a twist
Can't resist that movie list
But remember when you see that face
There's more than meets the eye in outer space!
(Oh Tom! Oh Tom!)
That crazy little problematic man!
```

**Techniques demonstrated**:
- Call-and-response in parentheses
- Consistent AABB rhyme scheme in verses
- Chorus repeats exactly
- Intro sets up the narrative hook
- Bridge shifts perspective

## Example 7: Philosophical Rap Battle

**Title**: Aristotle vs Wittgenstein: Is Quesadilla a Cheesecake?

**Style**: `Rap Battle, male vocals, aggressive, metaphysical`

```
[Verse 1 — Aristotle]

Prime mover on the mic, I define every form,
Essence in my Categories — that's the culinary norm.
Cheesecake: sweet custard, final cause = dessert,
Quesadilla: street snack, folded flat — that's alert.
Don't confuse the telos, I'm the logic OG,
Four causes bring order; you just play with family trees.

⸻

[Verse 2 — Late Wittgenstein]

Hold up, Ari-old-school, your marble mind's brittle —
I smash those rigid essences like queso in the griddle.
Meaning's in use, bro; check my Investigations,
Words work by practice, not cosmic foundations.
"Cheesecake," "quesadilla" — we play different games;
Family resemblances, overlapping names.

⸻

[Verse 3 — Slavoj Žižek]

ŽIŽEK here — I break through like cream filling oozing:
Both of you miss how ideology is choosing.
Cheesecake, quesadilla — objet petit cheese,
Desire curdled in pastry, capitalism's tease.
The Real is the goo that won't fit in a slice,
It drips, overflows — that's the chef's true device.
```

**Techniques demonstrated**:
- Multi-character format with labeled verses
- Dense internal rhymes
- Academic/philosophical vocabulary
- Divider lines between sections
- Unexpected third-party intervention (Žižek)
